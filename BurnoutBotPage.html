Burnout Bot
Art Installation

Artists' Statement: Burnout Bot is a mixed media work that represents the consequences of overworking oneself. Through the use of a game-like interface, the viewer is invited to click a button and earn points. However, as the points accumulate, the robot character begins to show signs of distress, from a happy face to a sad face to tears and eventually leaking oil. This representation of a machine breaking down under the weight of excessive labor is a commentary on the dangers of pushing oneself or others too hard in the pursuit of success. By exposing the negative effects of overwork, Burnout Bot invites viewers to consider the importance of work-life balance and the value of self-care.

My contribution to the project:

This project was created by myself and a partner. I wrote all the code as well as wired and tested the initial breadboard prototype of the electrical components. We worked together on the overall design and concept, the planning, and the final wiring (soldering and hot gluing wires to form the wire sculpture) and testing of the installation.
